Our Story

Your All-Round Building Solution

NPD Building Solutions is a carpentry and renovations business based in Bairnsdale, VIC, with over 10 years of hands-on experience. We started with a simple idea: deliver quality workmanship, pay attention to the details, and treat every job like it's our own home.

Over the years, that approach has earned us a strong word-of-mouth reputation across Bairnsdale and the wider East Gippsland region. We've grown from general carpentry into a full-service building solution, covering everything from decks and pergolas to tiling, fencing, gates, and complete bathroom renovations.

One of our proudest achievements has been completing 16 quality bathrooms in just 14 months alongside Cutting Edge Constructions. That kind of volume, combined with consistent quality, speaks to the reliability and standards we bring to every project.
