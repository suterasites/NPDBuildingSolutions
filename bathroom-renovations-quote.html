Questions

Frequently asked.

How long does a bathroom renovation take? +

Most full bathroom renovations take 3 to 4 weeks from strip-out to fit-out, depending on size and fixture lead times. You'll get a clear timeline with your quote.

How much does a bathroom renovation cost? +

Typical full renovations in East Gippsland range from $15,000 to $30,000 or more depending on size, fixtures and finishes. You get a fixed-price quote after a free site visit so there are no surprises.

Do you coordinate the plumber and electrician? +

Yes. We manage every trade involved so you don't have to chase anyone yourself. Plumbing, electrical, waterproofing, tiling and fit-out are all coordinated by one builder.

What's included in a full bathroom renovation? +

Strip-out, waterproofing, tiling, plumbing rough-in and fit-off, electrical, cabinetry, vanity, basin, toilet, shower and any custom carpentry. Everything from bare studs back to a finished bathroom.

Do I have to choose all the fixtures and tiles myself? +

You choose the look you want. We'll help with sourcing if it speeds things up, but you keep control of the design and budget. Nothing gets ordered without your sign-off.

Is the work guaranteed? +

Yes. We stand behind every bathroom we renovate. Workmanship guaranteed, waterproofing certified to Australian Standards, and we come back if anything is not right.

What areas do you service? +

Bairnsdale, Paynesville, Lakes Entrance, Sale, Maffra, Stratford, and surrounding East Gippsland and Wellington Shire areas.

How quickly can you start? +

We usually quote within 24 hours and can book most Bairnsdale bathroom renovations within 2 to 4 weeks, depending on the season and current bookings. Call 0427 278 285 to check current availability.
