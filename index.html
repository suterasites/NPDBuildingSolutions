What We Do

Our Services

From site prep to load outs, we cover all types of earthworks for residential, rural, and commercial projects.

Site Cuts & Cleans

Precision site preparation for residential and commercial builds. We cut and clean your site to spec, ready for the next stage of construction.

  • Residential & commercial site cuts
  • Site clearing & levelling
  • Concrete prep & pad work

Dam Cleans & Farm Tracks

Rural property services including dam restoration and farm access track construction. We keep your property functional and well-maintained year round.

  • Dam desilting & clean outs
  • Farm track construction & grading
  • Drainage & water management

Footings & Pier Holes

Accurate excavation for footings and pier holes to engineer specs. We work with builders and concreters to get foundations right the first time.

  • Strip & pad footings
  • Pier holes to spec
  • Trenching & service runs

Load Outs & Soil Spreading

Efficient material handling for dirt, rock, and concrete. Whether you need material moved on or off site, we handle the heavy lifting.

  • Dirt, rock & concrete load outs
  • Topsoil spreading & levelling
  • Material cartage & removal
