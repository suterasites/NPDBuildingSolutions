What We Do

Our Services

We stick to tile roofing and the services that naturally sit around it. That kind of focus means better results for your roof.

Tile Repairs

Cracked, broken or slipped tiles replaced quickly and properly. We match existing tiles to keep your roof looking consistent and watertight.

  • Cracked & broken tile replacement
  • Ridge cap repairs
  • Roof leak detection & repair

Rebedding & Repointing

Ridge capping mortar breaks down over time. We remove the old bedding, reset your ridge caps and seal them with flexible pointing compound for a lasting finish.

  • Full ridge cap rebedding
  • Flexible pointing compound
  • Mortar restoration

Full Roof Restorations

A complete roof overhaul - cleaning, repairs, rebedding and coating. We bring tired roofs back to life, adding years of protection and improving your home's street appeal.

  • High-pressure cleaning
  • Repairs, rebedding & repointing
  • Roof coating & sealing

Additional Services

Beyond tile work, we handle all the extras that keep your roof and home in top shape - from gutters and solar panels to bird proofing.

  • Gutter cleaning
  • Bird proofing & solar panel mesh
  • Solar panel cleaning
